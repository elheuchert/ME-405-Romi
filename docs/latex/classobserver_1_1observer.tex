\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
observer class that can be initialized to be run with the cotask scheduler. 

See run for behavior.

{\bfseries{init}} is the observer object initializer which takes 6 arguments. The arguments are data shares. 
\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em left\+\_\+pos} & \+: left\+\_\+pos is a float share that holds the current position of the left encoder. Used as an input. \\
\hline
{\em right\+\_\+pos} & \+: right\+\_\+pos is a float share that holds the current position of the right encoder. Used as an input. \\
\hline
{\em yaw} & \+: yaw is a float share that holds the IMU reading of the yaw. \\
\hline
{\em yaw\+\_\+velocity} & \+: yaw\+\_\+velocity is the yaw\+\_\+velocity as measured by the IMU task. \\
\hline
{\em left\+\_\+voltage} & \+: left\+\_\+voltage is the PWM value of the left motor which observer scales to the expected voltage. \\
\hline
{\em right\+\_\+voltage} & \+: right\+\_\+voltage is the PWM value of the right motor which observer scales to the expected voltage. \\
\hline
{\em left\+\_\+vel} & \+: left\+\_\+vel is the velocity of the left motor. \\
\hline
{\em right\+\_\+vel} & \+: right\+\_\+vel is the velocity of the right motor. \\
\hline
{\em total\+\_\+dist} & \+: total\+\_\+dist is a float share that the observer will fill with how far it thinks ROMI has travelled in mm. \\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
